    Your instructors and mentors

    Learning software development on your own is tough. At WBS CODING SCHOOL you can be sure to have the full support of your classmates and instructors. Each class has an assigned full-time lead instructor and additional teaching assistants, depending on the class size. There is always someone to help you when you need it.

    A unique hybrid course structure with 2 phases

    Live online learning | 12 weeks

    For the first 12 weeks you study remotely on our virtual learning platform together with your classmates. So you decide from where to join. Live and interactive coding classes with instructor-led lectures, workshops, team coding challenges and much more.

    Learning in Berlin | 3 weeks

    The big finale is your stay at our Campus Berlin – accommodation and food included. You live and code together with your classmates on your final portfolio project. You receive personal career coaching, we organize visits to tech-community events, trips to explore the exciting Berlin tech ecosystem and make sure that we have a great time together.
